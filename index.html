Our Vision

We are a multi-diciplinary team of engineers at UConn passionate about making an impact on both our local Storrs community and our world. Ambulances can only travel so fast and unforeseen traffic or remote locations can take a long time to reach when people are panicking and professional advice is vital. This is where we step in. Drones are very cheap to deploy and can cut the response time drastically when having attention is most vital. Our system enables ER doctors to get eyes on the situation at lightning speed to calm patients and guide them through necessary action all the while keeping ambulances informed creating a seamless experience when you need it most.
